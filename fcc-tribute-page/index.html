Leonardo da Vinci

known for anatomy, cartography, painting, and palaeontology and regarded as "Renaissance Man" or "Universal Genius".

Timeline of Leonardo da Vinci:

  • 1452 -Born in the Tuscan hill town of Vinci
  • 1475 -Paints the Annunciation
  • 1476 -Paints the Portrait of Ginevra de' Benci
  • 1478 -Paints The Benois Madonna
  • 1480 -Paints the Madonna od the Carnation
  • 1480 -Sketches the Self-Propelled Cart
  • 1480 -Paints St. Jerome in the Wilderness
  • 1483 -Sketches the Design for Parachute
  • 1485 -Sketches the Design for a Giant Crossbow
  • 1485 -Sketches the Design for a Scythed Chariot
  • 1485 -Sketches the Design for a Tank
  • 1487 -Draws the Vitruvian Man
  • 1489 -Sketches the Armored Vessel
  • 1488 -Designs his ideal City
  • 1489 -Sketches the Design for Flying Machine
  • 1490 -Sketches the Design for a Double Decker Bridge
  • 1490 -Sketches the Design for an Adding Machine
  • 1505 -Writes his Codex on the Flight of Birds
  • 1493 -Sketches the Design for the "Aerial Screw"
  • 1498 -Paints the Last Supper
  • 1500 -Sketches Design for a Machine Gun
  • 1502 -Sketches the Design for the Golden Horn Bridge
  • 1506 -Paints the Famous Mona Lisa
  • 1505 -Paints The battle of Anghiari, known as the "Lost Leonardo"
  • 1519 -Dies in Amboise.
